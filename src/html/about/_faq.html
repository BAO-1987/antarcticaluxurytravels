Frequently Asked Questions: Your Queries Answered

  • What is included in the tour price?

    Our tour prices include accommodations, meals, guided excursions, and all necessary equipment. International flights, travel insurance, and personal expenses are not included.

  • How should I prepare for the extreme weather conditions in Antarctica?

    We provide a detailed packing list to help you prepare. Essential items include layered clothing, waterproof outerwear, and sturdy boots. Our team is available to offer additional guidance on gear and preparation.

  • What safety measures are in place for the tours?

    Safety is our top priority. Our expeditions are led by experienced guides trained in first aid and emergency response. We also carry comprehensive medical kits and have protocols in place for various emergency situations.
